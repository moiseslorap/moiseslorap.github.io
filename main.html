About Me


Hola! I'm Moisés Lora, a third-year student at the Rochester Institute of Technology currently pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Software Engineering, with minors in Mobile Development & Computing Security. I would describe myself as a hardworking, dedicated, responsible individual with a passion for technology and coding. Enthusiast for overcoming challenges and obstacles in life. Bilingual in English & Spanish, with strong leadership and interpersonal skills looking to grow and succeed in all aspects of life.

I originally hail from the Dominican Republic, where I was born and raised. Where ever since my middle school years, I have always had an immense passion about technology and have always envisioned myself with the goal of creating new software that would make millions of lives easier one day. With this goal set in mind, I put myself through years of hardwork in Bilingual School which led to many acceptance letters and scholarship offers from many of my top schools. I decided on attending the Rochester Institute of Technology, as it offered both a competitive financial aid package and a top ranked Software Engineering Program.

Since making it to RIT, I have been working hard on achieving my dreams regardless of the obstacles that have come my way. In my spare time, I try to maintain a healthy lifestyle by working out and playing sports, some occasional latin dancing as well as working on my personal projects. Feel free to take a look at this website and learn more about me and what I do!!
